conform-ed gives engineers **typed contracts** to produce standards-correct data, a **headless QTI
runtime** to deliver assessments, and **conformance runners** to prove an implementation conforms —
across xAPI, QTI, LTI 1.3, Common Cartridge, OneRoster, CASE, CLR, Open Badges, Caliper, and more.

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Standards & contracts" icon="document">
    Zod validators for ~13 standards in `@conform-ed/contracts`. [Browse the standards →](/contracts/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="QTI delivery runtime" icon="puzzle">
    Headless, accessible QTI 3 delivery with `@conform-ed/qti-react` and `qti-xml`. [Deliver QTI →](/qti/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Conformance runners" icon="approve-check-circle">

  </Card>

  </Card>
