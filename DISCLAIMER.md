## No certification

conform-ed produces **conformance assessments** and **interop evidence**; these
are **not official certification**. Only the relevant standards body can certify
conformance to its specification.

A passing conform-ed result indicates only that an implementation satisfied the
specific, scoped checks conform-ed performed — see each spec's **Coverage Map**
for the exact denominator. It is **not** a certification, warranty, or guarantee
of conformance, interoperability, or fitness for any purpose. Results produced by
the `oss-*` interop lanes are **interop evidence against a real but unverified
counterpart**, not a conformance verdict — the counterpart is not assumed to be
conformant itself.
